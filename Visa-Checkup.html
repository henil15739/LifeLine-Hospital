Immigrants Visa Health Checkup

We provide certified medical examinations and reports required for immigration and visa purposes. Fast processing, private consultations and clear documentation for consulates.

What is included
  • Medical examination by qualified physician
  • Chest X-Ray (if required)
  • Basic blood tests: CBC, RBS
  • Serology tests (HIV / Hepatitis) when requested
  • Urine analysis and general physical fitness report
Important notes

- Different countries require different tests. Please confirm required checklist before appointment.

- Bring original ID and previous medical records if any.

- Fasting may be required for some blood tests; confirm at booking.
