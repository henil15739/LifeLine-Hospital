Important Guidelines

  • Please take a prior appointment. Bring urine/stool containers if required.
  • Plan 4–6 hours for the check-up depending on the package.
  • Confirm fasting requirements with Customer Care (usually 8–12 hours).
  • Wear loose clothing and avoid jewellery. Bring previous medical records.
  • Some tests (e.g. Pap smear) may be available the next day. Inform us if pregnant or menstruating.
